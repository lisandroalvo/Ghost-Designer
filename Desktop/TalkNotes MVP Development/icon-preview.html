Design Principles

  • 1.5px stroke weight for soft, non-intrusive presence
  • Rounded caps and joins for gentle, flowing aesthetic
  • Layered opacity creates depth without visual weight
  • Abstract metaphors: breath, balance, growth, flow
  • Monochrome with currentColor for contextual adaptation
  • Subtle glow effect with soft green accent (#86efac)
  • Inspired by meditation apps and mindful tech design
  • Icons enhance focus rather than drawing attention
